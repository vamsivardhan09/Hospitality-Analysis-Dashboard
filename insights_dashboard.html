Critical findings
  • !
    June revenue dropped 4.8% — driven by bookings (-4.8%), occupancy (-1.6%) and unutilized capacity (+2.3%). Cancellation rates were stable, so it was a demand problem, not a retention problem.
  • !
    Atliq Seasons is a critical failure. Rating 2.29, occupancy only 44.62%, 55.38% unutilized capacity. Operates only in Mumbai. Cancellations (24.79%) + no-shows (4.62%) compound the damage.
  • !
    Week 3 occupancy dip across all months. Consistent pattern — mid-month demand weakness suggests seasonal/behavioral pattern not yet addressed by pricing strategy.
  • !
    Week 32 collapse. Capacity dropped sharply, causing bookings and revenue to fall dramatically. Supply-side limitation in final week.
Positive signals
  • +
    Weekend occupancy at 73.58% — 22 points above weekday. Weekend demand is strong. Dynamic pricing on weekends could significantly lift revenue.
  • +
    Atliq Blu leads all properties in occupancy (62.02%) with a strong rating of 3.96. Best-performing asset overall — a model for others.
  • +
    Bangalore is a revenue efficiency leader. Despite lowest occupancy, it generates 2nd highest revenue with lowest cancellation rate — high-value, committed guests.
  • ~
    Luxury category dominates at 61.61% of revenue. Business hotels underperform at 38.39%. Investment in luxury positioning yields measurable returns.

Channel strategy insights
  • !
    40.93% from untracked sources. This is priority #1 for analytics investment — could be corporate accounts, walk-ins, or dark traffic from OTAs not properly tagged.
  • 2
    MakeYourTrip is the only identified OTA leader at 19.95%. Strong negotiating position but also dependency risk — diversification recommended.
  • +
    Direct online at 9.89% — growing this channel reduces OTA commission costs (typically 15–25%). Even 5% shift = significant margin improvement.
  • ~
    Journey (6%) and Tripster (7.2%) are small contributors. Worth evaluating ROI on maintaining these partnerships vs. concentrating on top performers.
Recommended actions
Resolve attribution gap
Implement UTM tracking + CRM tagging to identify the 40.93% unknown source. This is likely the largest hidden revenue stream.
Boost direct bookings
Loyalty program + best-rate guarantee on owned channels. Every 1% shift from OTA to direct saves ~₹1.5M in commission fees.
Fix cancellation leakage
Strict no-show policy + pre-arrival check-in messages. 11.66% cancellation + 5.88% no-show = 17.54% booking waste.
Fix Atliq Seasons urgently
Rating 2.29 is unacceptable for a 5-star brand. Service audit + staff retraining + occupancy incentives needed immediately.
